Why Explore Downtown LA

A living record of
the city's growth.

Downtown Los Angeles is one of the city's oldest and most historically significant areas. It is home to government buildings, cultural institutions, public spaces, and architectural landmarks that reflect Los Angeles's growth, design, and identity over time.

The area includes buildings that serve practical, civic, and cultural purposes while also showcasing distinctive materials, forms, and design features.

01

Government & Civic

Courthouses, headquarters, and public buildings designed with intention and purpose.

02

Cultural institutions

Museums, concert halls, and schools that anchor the area's cultural identity.

03

Distinctive materials & form

From honeycomb facades to curved steel waves, design choices that reward a second look.

More to Explore in Downtown LA

Architecture is only part of the experience. Downtown LA also offers food, markets, nightlife, and cultural attractions.

Grand Central Market

A historic food hall filled with local flavors and city energy.

Smorgasburg LA

An open-air market where food, shopping, and entertainment come together.

LA Live

A vibrant downtown hub featuring sports, restaurants, and premier entertainment venues.
